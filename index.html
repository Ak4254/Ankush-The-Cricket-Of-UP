About

The Player

I'm Ankush Kumar, an opening batsman for the CSJM University First XI. I picked up a bat at age eight and have spent every season since chasing the next century — currently sitting on four.

My game is built on solid footwork against spin and a strong cover drive, and I take pride in batting deep into an innings rather than chasing quick runs early. Off the field, I lead the team's net practice sessions and mentor the junior batting group.

Currently captaining the school's U-19 squad for the upcoming inter-school championship season.
